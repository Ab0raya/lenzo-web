Comprehensive Optical Data

Get a complete set of measurements required for advanced progressive and single vision lens fitting.

Pupillary Distance (PD)

Monocular Left & Right PD measurements for precise optical centration.

Fitting Heights

Vertical height from the pupil center to the bottom of the lens.

Near PD

Pupillary distance calculated for reading / near vision tasks.

Vertex Distance

Distance between the back surface of the lens and the apex of the cornea.

Pantoscopic Angle

The angle of inclination of the frame front relative to the face.

Wrap Angle

Curvature of the frame front, essential for high-base curve lenses.

Frame Dimensions

A-Box (Width) and B-Box (Height) dimensions of the lens shape.

Distance Between Lenses (DBL)

Bridge width measurement between the two lenses.
